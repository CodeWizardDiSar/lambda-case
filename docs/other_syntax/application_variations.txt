
type application:

  - (:->/<-:/--> and <--) variations:

    A, B :-> TypeName
    TypeName <-: A, B
    A-->TypeName<--B

  - one parenthesis in the middle variation
    (TypeName and MoreTypeName must have at least one character)

    TypeName(A, B)MoreTypeName

  - seperate parentheses in the middle variation
    (TypeName, MoreTypeName and EvenMoreTypeName must have at least one character)

    TypeName(A)MoreTypeName(B)EvenMoreTypeName

combination of variations example:

  A-->TypeName(B, C)MoreTypeName(D)EvenMoreTypeName <-: E, F

same for function application:

  - (:->/<-:/--> and <--) variations:

    a, b :-> f
    f <-: A, B
    a-->f<--b

  - one parenthesis in the middle variation
    (f_name_start and more_f_name must have at least one character)

    f_name_start(A, B)more_f_name

  - seperate parentheses in the middle variation
    (f_name_start, more_f_name and even_more_f_name must have at least one character)

    f_name_start(A)more_f_name(B)even_more_f_name
